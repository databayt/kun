# 1:1 — Abdout (Self-Review)

> A founder doesn't have a manager. The 1:1 with Abdout is the founder's own structured self-review.
> Cadence: weekly (Friday review section), monthly (founder retrospective), quarterly (deeper).
> Source: Marshall Goldsmith — "What Got You Here Won't Get You There" + Andrew Bosworth on career conversations.

---

## Standing prep questions (every weekly self-review)

1. **What did I do this week that worked?** (Goldsmith feedforward — do more of this)
2. **What did I avoid this week that I shouldn't have?** (the bottleneck the founder is creating)
3. **Where did I add too much value?** (Goldsmith bad habit #2 — telling teammates how to do their job better instead of letting them do it)
4. **Where did I win an argument I should have lost?** (Goldsmith bad habit #1 — winning too much)
5. **What is the most important thing I am NOT doing right now?**
6. **Who needs my time more than they're getting it?**
7. **What's the next Type-1 decision approaching?**

---

## Standing prep questions (every monthly review)

1. **What pattern showed up this month that's worth promoting to PRINCIPLES.md?**
2. **What story am I telling myself that might not be true?** (Annie Duke — sharpen beliefs)
3. **Default alive or default dead?** (Graham — calculate honestly)
4. **What's my energy level out of 10?** (Horowitz — manage your psychology)
5. **What's a piece of feedback I haven't given that I should have?**
6. **What's a piece of feedback I should ask for?**

---

## Standing prep questions (quarterly)

1. **Run the keeper test on each teammate** (silently — entries below). For each: "If they told me they were leaving for a similar role at a competitor, would I fight hard to keep them?"
2. **What's a habit I had this quarter I want to keep?**
3. **What's a habit I had this quarter I want to drop?**
4. **What's a 2-month-old decision that I should now reverse?**
5. **What's the most important thing I learned this quarter?**

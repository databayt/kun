Plan and review the content calendar.

Arguments: $ARGUMENTS (optional: "plan" for next week, "review" for this week, or a specific date range)

Steps:
1. Check what products are shipping soon (product agent context)
2. Check recent competitor moves (analyst agent context)
3. Review growth agent's content pillars:
   - Arabic-first tech content (Samia)
   - Product marketing per vertical
   - Developer relations (Kun, open source)
   - Founder story
4. Generate weekly content plan:
   - Monday: Product content (Arabic)
   - Tuesday: Technical blog/tutorial
   - Wednesday: Community engagement
   - Thursday: Case study / social proof
   - Friday: Developer relations
5. For each piece, provide:
   - Title (Arabic + English)
   - Target platform (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Dev.to, GitHub)
   - Author: Samia (content), Ali (business), Abdout (technical)
   - SEO keywords (Arabic + English)
   - Brief (2-3 sentences)
6. Output as markdown calendar

# Mkan — Social

*"Saudi property rentals, simplified" — Mkan for homes.* A rental marketplace, Airbnb-style. This
is the most **visual, consumer-first** brand in the portfolio: the [SaaS](/docs/social#business-models--who-were-talking-to)
product sells to two sides — **B2C** renters/travelers looking for a place, and **B2B** hosts
listing one. Social leans hard into imagery and short-form video.

## Positioning

- **Business model:** SaaS marketplace (two-sided).
- **Type:** B2C (renters) + B2B (hosts / property managers).
- **Tagline:** Saudi property rentals, simplified.
- **Angle:** beautiful places, booked in a tap — and effortless for hosts.

## Audience

| Segment | Side | What moves them |
|---------|------|-----------------|
| **Renters / travelers** | Demand (B2C) | Gorgeous listings, trust, easy booking, good prices |
| **Hosts / owners** | Supply (B2B) | Bookings filled, low hassle, fair fees |
| **Property managers** | Supply (B2B) | Manage many units in one place |

## Channel mix

Mkan is a photo-and-video product. Instagram, TikTok, and Snapchat carry it; Facebook and X
support. This is the brand where the consumer-visual channels finally lead.

| Priority | Channel | Why |
|----------|---------|-----|
| **Primary** | **Instagram** | Property showcases, Reels, stories — the natural home |
| **Primary** | **TikTok**, **Snapchat** | Short property tours; huge MENA/Saudi reach |
| **Secondary** | **Facebook** | Marketplace crossover, host community, older renters |
| **Secondary** | **X**, **Telegram** | Deals, new-listing drops, community |
| **Low** | LinkedIn | B2B host acquisition only |

## Content pillars

1. **Property showcases** — the hero content. Text-free `/higgs` imagery + real listing photos;
   Reels/tours on IG/TikTok/Snapchat.
2. **Booking made easy** — the "tap to book" story; trust and reviews.
3. **Host success** — "listed on Monday, booked by Friday." Recruits supply.
4. **Saudi destinations & lifestyle** — chalets, getaways, seasonal travel — inspiration that sells.

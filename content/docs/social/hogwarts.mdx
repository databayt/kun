# Hogwarts — Social

Modern school management for Saudi / MENA schools — *"schools automate the mundane."* Hogwarts is
the flagship [SaaS](/docs/social#business-models--who-were-talking-to) product: the sale is **B2B**
(school owners and administrators buy it), but the reach is **B2C** (parents and teachers see it
every day, and their trust closes the sale). Social has to speak to both.

## Positioning

- **Business model:** SaaS.
- **Type:** B2B sale · B2C social proof.
- **Tagline:** Modern school management for Saudi / MENA schools.
- **Angle:** give administrators their time back; give parents visibility and calm.

## Audience

| Segment | Role | What moves them |
|---------|------|-----------------|
| **School owners / administrators** | The buyers (B2B) | Time saved, fewer errors, billing/attendance handled, compliance |
| **Teachers** | Daily users | Less admin, easier grading/attendance |
| **Parents** | Reach + trust (B2C) | Visibility into their child, timely communication |

## Channel mix

Parents and school communities in MENA live on Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp; the buyers are
reachable on LinkedIn. That's the core four.

| Priority | Channel | Why |
|----------|---------|-----|
| **Primary** | **LinkedIn** | Reach the decision-makers — owners, principals, education buyers |
| **Primary** | **Facebook**, **Instagram** | Where parents and school communities are; visual proof, announcements |
| **Primary** | **WhatsApp** | School↔parent communication is already WhatsApp — broadcast lists, updates |
| **Secondary** | **X**, **Telegram** | Announcements, education-tech conversation |
| **Low** | TikTok, Snapchat | Occasional light content; not the buyer's channel |

## Content pillars

1. **School success stories** — a school that cut admission or billing time; real numbers, with
   permission. The B2B proof that also reassures parents.
2. **Feature highlights** — attendance, grades, billing, SIS — each shown solving one concrete
   headache. Short demo clips (`/higgs` media).
3. **Saudi / MENA education context** — term calendars, regulator realities, Arabic-first.
4. **Administrator time-savings** — "an hour of paperwork, done in a click."

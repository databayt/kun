# Moallimee — Social

> **Pre-launch.** Moallimee (معلمي — "my teacher") has a CRM workspace live at
> [moallimee.crm.databayt.org](https://moallimee.crm.databayt.org) but no public product or repo
> yet. This page is a **scaffold**: the channel and pillar choices below are proposals to confirm
> when the product's positioning is locked. Nothing here should ship as fact before launch.

## Positioning *(proposed)*

- **Business model:** SaaS (marketplace, likely two-sided).
- **Type:** B2C (students / parents) + B2B (teachers / tutors).
- **Working angle:** connect students with the right teacher — tutoring, made simple.
- **Confirm at launch:** exact scope (tutoring marketplace vs. school-teacher tooling vs. LMS),
  primary market, and tagline. Until then, treat pillars as hypotheses.

## Audience *(proposed)*

| Segment | Side | Likely driver |
|---------|------|----------------|
| **Students** | Demand (B2C) | Find help fast, pass the exam, learn a skill |
| **Parents** | Demand (B2C) | A trustworthy tutor for their child |
| **Teachers / tutors** | Supply (B2B) | Students, income, a professional profile |

## Channel mix *(proposed)*

Education-consumer product → student-heavy channels lead, with parent and messaging channels
close behind.

| Priority | Channel | Why *(proposed)* |
|----------|---------|------------------|
| **Primary** | **Instagram**, **TikTok** | Where students are; study tips, tutor spotlights, short lessons |
| **Primary** | **WhatsApp** | Tutor↔student coordination (messaging, not feed posts) |
| **Secondary** | **Snapchat** | Student reach in Saudi/MENA |
| **Secondary** | **Facebook** | Parents |
| **Low** | X, LinkedIn | Teacher recruitment / thought leadership only |

## Content pillars *(proposed)*

1. **Learning tips & micro-lessons** — bite-size, exam-season aware, Arabic first.
2. **Teacher spotlights** — recruit supply and build trust.
3. **Student wins** — social proof once the product is live.
4. **Exam-prep seasons** — align content to the academic calendar.

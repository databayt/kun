# Databayt — Social

The parent brand. Databayt's own presence tells the company story, not a single product's: *"we
automate the boring, in the open, and everyone who contributes owns their share."* This is the
B2B and developer-relations voice — it serves three of the four [business
models](/docs/social#business-models--who-were-talking-to): **Per Project, Partners, and
Codebase**. The SaaS products get their own [brand pages](/docs/social#brand-pages).

## Positioning

- **Business models:** Per Project (custom builds), Partners (outsourced dev + talent), Codebase
  (contributor revenue-share — the sharing economy).
- **Type:** B2B + developer relations.
- **One line:** *"How we run a software company with Claude Code — open source, contribution-owned."*
- **Strongest pitch:** the open-source, sharing-economy story. Lead with it.

## Audience

| Segment | What they want | What we show them |
|---------|----------------|-------------------|
| **Developers / contributors** | Interesting code, a way to earn from it | Open-source work, the revenue-share model, Kun internals |
| **Businesses seeking automation** | Proof we can automate their boring | Case studies, "before/after" automation stories |
| **Partners & talent** | A serious team to build with | Founder story, the 7-person + AI-agents operating model |
| **The MENA tech community** | Arabic-first technical content | Tutorials, build logs, entrepreneurship in Arabic |

## Channel mix

Databayt is developer- and business-facing, so it leans on the professional and technical
channels — not the consumer-visual ones.

| Priority | Channel | Why |
|----------|---------|-----|
| **Primary** | **X**, **LinkedIn** | Dev-rel + product launches (X); B2B, partners, talent (LinkedIn) |
| **Primary** | **GitHub**, **Dev.to** | The open-source home; long-form technical posts |
| **Secondary** | **Telegram** | Community/announcements broadcast |
| **Low** | Instagram, TikTok, Snapchat, Facebook | Consumer channels — occasional founder/culture content only |

## Content pillars

1. **The open-source story** — "automate the boring," every line open, contribution-owned. The
   sharing-economy model in practice.
2. **"How we run a company with Claude Code"** — the Kun engine, agents, the operating model. Our
   sharpest differentiator and best dev-rel hook.
3. **Founder & team story** — building in Saudi/Sudan, electrical-engineering → software, a small
   team amplified by AI agents.
4. **Product launches & milestones** — announce each SaaS product's news from the umbrella too.

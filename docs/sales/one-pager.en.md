# Databayt — School Management Platform

**One system to run your whole school — from admission to the end-of-year report.**

A cloud, open-source, Arabic-first (RTL) platform built for schools in Sudan and the Gulf. Every branch, every class, every student — in one place.

---

## What Databayt solves

Schools run on paper registers, scattered spreadsheets, and apps that don't talk to each other — so data goes missing, fees go uncollected, and parents go uninformed. Databayt brings it together:

- **Admissions** — full online application journey, admin review, status tracking.
- **Attendance** — daily, multi-mode, with instant parent alerts.
- **Exams & grades** — build exams, record marks, produce report cards.
- **Timetables** — auto-generated schedules.
- **Finance** — fees, invoices, payroll, and collection in one place.
- **Parent portal** — messaging, child progress, grades, downloadable reports.
- **Live classes** — live video lessons students join with one button.
- **Messaging & notifications** — over email and WhatsApp.

All in Arabic (and English), with multi-branch support.

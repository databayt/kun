## 2. Territory

The Salesperson is engaged to source and close school customers in **Sudan and the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia**. Deals outside this territory require written pre-approval.

## 3. Definitions

- **Landed School** — a school that has (a) signed a Databayt service contract **and** (b) made its **first payment**, which the Company has **collected**. A verbal commitment, a signed letter of intent, or a started pilot is **not** "landed".
- **First-Year Revenue** — the net revenue the Company actually collects from that school during the **12 months** following its first payment, excluding taxes, refunds, and chargebacks.
- **Collection** — funds cleared into a Company account (not merely invoiced).

## 4. Commission

| The Salesperson's…                | Commission on First-Year Revenue |
| --------------------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| **1st through 5th** Landed School | **50%**                          |
| **6th** Landed School onward      | **30%**                          |

- The tier is a **lifetime count** of the Salesperson's Landed Schools — the 6th deal steps down to 30% and stays there.
- Commission is on **First-Year Revenue only**. **No commission accrues on renewals** (year 2 and beyond), even if the school stays for years.
- Each Landed School has its **own independent 12-month** First-Year-Revenue window.

> **CONFIRM before signing:** the source strategy says "new deals from the same salesperson restart the commission cycle." This template reads that as _each new school opens its own fresh 12-month window_ (above), with the 50%→30% tier as a lifetime count. If the intent was different (e.g. the 5-school tier resets per period), correct this clause before use.

## 5. Payment mechanics

- Commission is paid **within 30 days** of the Company **collecting** the relevant school payment.
- If a school **churns before 12 months**, commission is recalculated as the tier % of **revenue actually collected** to the churn date (pro-rated), and any overpayment is reconciled against future commission.
- Payments are made by [bank transfer / agreed method] in [currency].

## 6. Conditions

- Commission is earned **only** on a Landed School (signed **and** first payment collected) — never on pipeline, demos, or pilots alone.
- The Company sets pricing and contract terms with the school. The Salesperson does not have authority to bind the Company.

## 7. Confidentiality

Commission terms, pricing, the customer pipeline, and product internals are confidential. The Salesperson shares **none** of this with prospect schools or third parties. The prospect-facing one-pager is the only customer-shareable collateral.

## 8. Term & termination

Either party may end this engagement with [N] days' written notice. Commission already **earned** (on Landed Schools, within their 12-month windows) survives termination and is paid out per §5. No new commission accrues after the end date.

## 9. Dispute resolution

Disputes over whether a school is "landed", over revenue figures, or over payment are resolved first by good-faith discussion, then by [mediation / the agreed forum]. The Company's collection records are the reference for First-Year Revenue.
